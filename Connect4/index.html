Welcome to the exhilarating world of Connect4!

Objective: Be the first to line up four of your colored discs vertically, horizontally, or diagonally while outwitting your opponent with lightning-fast moves. But here's the twist: you've got to be quick on your feet because there's a timer ticking away, daring you to make your move before it's your opponent's turn! With every second counting, can you outsmart your rival and achieve victory in this high-speed battle of wits?

Get ready to drop, block, and connect your way to glory!
